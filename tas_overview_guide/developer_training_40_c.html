Developer training

HPE TAS-specific developer training is available, with a predefined curriculum. The curriculum can be customized based on customer requirements.

The HPE TAS developer training can be delivered via a face-to-face workshop at the customer's site or at an HPE site by a senior Technical Consultant. The training includes hands-on exercises. Face-to-face training is the most recommended, due to its higher effectiveness. Alternatively, the training could also be customized to be delivered online. The default face-to-face training curriculum is a 5-day training.

Learning objectives of the default training are:

  • Understand the product concepts and architecture
  • Apply best practices to design-efficient applications
  • Understand product APIs
  • Use appropriate tools to develop applications
  • Deploy developed applications on test systems

This course is offered on demand. A six to eight week lead time is required to schedule the training session.
