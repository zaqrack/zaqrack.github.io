Analyze network traffic - Wireshark and Tshark

Wireshark is a free, open-source network protocol analyzer. Wireshark shows what is happening on a network in great detail. It has the ability to decode a very wide range of protocols, including the ability to perform live-capture or work with precaptured information for offline analysis. Captured network data can be browsed via a GUI (Wireshark), or via the TTY-mode (TShark) utility.

Whireshark supports powerful features such as display filters, capture filters (when built-in traffic capture is used), coloring rules, capability to perform a call flow analysis, apart from the ability to not only work with compressed data, but also support decryption for many protocols, including IPsec, ISAKMP, Kerberos, SNMPv3, SSL/TLS, WEP, and WPA/WPA2.

More information can be found at https://www.wireshark.org/.
