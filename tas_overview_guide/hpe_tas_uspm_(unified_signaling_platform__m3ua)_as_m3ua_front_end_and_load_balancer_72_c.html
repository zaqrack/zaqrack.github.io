HPE TAS USPM (Unified Signaling Platform – M3UA) as M3UA front-end and load balancer

HPE TAS USPM (Unified Signaling Platform M3UA) is the M3UA front-end and load balancer for HPE TAS. It is a mandatory component of every deployment that needs to handle M3UA traffic over TCAP such as CAP, MAP, and INAP traffic.
