Product benefits

HPE TAS affords a long list of features that translate into a very credible set of benefits that make it an ideal platform to consolidate and converge a range of service-layer applications, bringing about holistic VAS transformation to facilitate significant operational cost-savings, vastly improve innovation agility, achieve a better compliance to regulatory requirements while being highly ready for future evolutions.

Following is a list of benefits of HPE TAS as a product:

  • Wide range of telecom network protocols and other IT protocols, permitting development of a very wide range of applications including call control, messaging, interactive multimedia solutions and various interworking-functions, gateways.
  • Powerful and expressive range of APIs at various levels of abstraction enabling faster ramp up and higher productivity for application developers.
  • Expressive, flexible and high-performance service orchestration allowing for service-blending of existing or new applications, permitting the creation of a new service experience with minimum effort, improving innovation agility.
  • Strong IMS support out-of-the-box, reducing risks to complex IMS projects.
  • Capabilities to perform service monitoring and control for charging & accounting, ensuring the possibility of a simple service monetization.
  • Application development in Java allows leverage of a very large set of third party libraries for faster development, reduced effort/cost and quicker time-to-market.
  • Application development using REST APIs for flexible integration between platform and application.
  • Application development workflow that is highly amenable to Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery.
  • High-performance execution environment offering high throughput and low latency.
  • Highly secure execution environment meeting most modern security requirement.
  • Runs virtualized for most optimal usage of underlying hardware resources.
  • Supported on HPE Proliant server series and any other appropriate hardware based on Intel Xeon processors with virtualization, expanding choices and avoiding any vendor lockdown.
  • Available for deployment in various NFV environments based on OpenStack with minor customizations and integration with MANO from various vendors.
  • Standard and common mechanisms for platform operations & monitoring, making OSS integration a simple and straight-forward task.
  • Integration support with a wide range of equipment operator OSSs, BSSs, government regulator equipments, and enterprise IT equipments making the platform a swiss-army-knife for telecom solutions.
  • Readily available applications ready to deploy or ready for customizations.
  • Strong expertise and excellent track-record of delivering solutions based on HPE TAS to customers sold as platform, sold as part of other HPE products, and complex solutions.
  • Structure partner support and enablement available.
  • Strong support capabilities in most geographies, with follow-the-sun support model, available for a range of SLOs to fit every requirement and every budget.

In summary, HPE TAS is the one single Telecom Application Server product that affords the best combination of features, performance, flexibility, compliance and readiness for a modern data center meeting the needs of today’s networks, potentially future networks and certainly most legacy networks.
