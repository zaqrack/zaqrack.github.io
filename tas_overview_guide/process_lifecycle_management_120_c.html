Process lifecycle management

HPE TAS uses systemd provided by the operating environment to start, stop, and monitor various HPE TAS processes. The operating environment-provided systemctl command is used as a CLI tool to perform operations to start, stop and check status of various components embodied as processes at runtime. The various commands applicable to various HPE TAS components are enlisted in the HPE TAS Operation, Administration, and Maintenance Guide.
